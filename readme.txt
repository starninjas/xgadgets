This mod is good for any PvP world!
It adds:
 -Fire Bottle - Throw it to make a trap of fire!
 -Explosive Bottle - Throw it and make an explosion!
 -Net Bottle - Throw to make sticky net traps!
 -Smoke Bottle - Creates a pillar of smoke! Perfect for escaping
 -Net Trap - Slows enemies down.
 -Phantom Stone - A block that looks like stone, but you can go through it! Perfect for hidden bases and traps.
Hope you enjoy this mod!
